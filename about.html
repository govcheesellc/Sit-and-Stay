Meet Bailee Williams

Hi there! I'm Bailee Williams, the founder and heart behind Sit & Stay Pet Care. Based in Allen, Texas, I've dedicated myself to providing exceptional, personalized pet care services to families in our community and surrounding areas within a 10-mile radius of ZIP code 75002.

My passion for animals began at an early age, and over the years, I've developed a deep understanding of what pets need to feel safe, comfortable, and loved when their families are away. Whether it's a quick daily check-in or an extended overnight stay, I treat every pet as if they were my own.
