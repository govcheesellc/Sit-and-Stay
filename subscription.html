Subscription Services

Coming Soon: Regular Pet Care Subscriptions

What Are Subscription Services?

We're working on developing subscription-based pet care services that will allow you to secure regular, ongoing care for your pets at discounted rates. These services will be perfect for busy pet parents who need consistent, reliable care on a weekly or monthly basis.

Planned Subscription Options

Future Subscription Plans:

  • Weekly Drop-In Package: Regular weekly visits at a discounted rate
  • Bi-Weekly Care Package: Every other week scheduled visits
  • Monthly Overnight Package: Monthly overnight stays for regular travelers
  • Senior Pet Care Package: Specialized care for older pets needing extra attention
  • Puppy Care Package: Frequent visits for young pets needing training and socialization

Benefits of Subscription Services

Subscription services will offer several advantages over individual bookings:

  • Cost Savings: Discounted rates compared to individual service bookings
  • Priority Scheduling: Guaranteed availability for your preferred time slots
  • Consistency: The same trusted caregiver for your pet every visit
  • Convenience: Automatic scheduling without the need to book each visit
  • Flexibility: Easy to modify or pause your subscription as needed

Get Notified

We're currently developing these subscription services and expect to launch them soon. If you're interested in being among the first to know when subscription services become available, please contact us at (817) 395-9982 or through our booking form.

In the meantime, we're happy to help you schedule regular services through our standard booking process.
